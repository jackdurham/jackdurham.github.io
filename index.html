About

In my previous job, I worked as a stock controller for the Fire and Rescue Service. I eventually decided to have some time for myself and travel across America for six months, where I spent the majority of my time in Seattle, during my time there I learnt to become more independent. While there, I found out about coding bootcamps, a new opportunity to pursue a career I’ve always dreamed of, so when I came back to London, I decided to join the 3-month Web Development Immersive programme at General Assembly.

I have always been interested in how computers and technology worked and I’ve felt that it was the right industry for me. I love the creative side of development and being able to add my own flair and personality to something out in the world for others to see and being able to solve problems is a huge, fun part of the process for me.

I’m looking to be part of a collaborative environment where I can continue to develop my skills throughout my career and feel excited to go to work and be in the offices.
